1. Personal Information We Collect

We collect your personal information when you access our website, create your account, response to a survey and do payment for purchase.
Personal information may include the following:
  • Name
  • Age
  • Address
  • Phonenumber
  • Email address

2. How We Use Your Personal Information

We will only use your personal information once you agree this Pricacy Policy.
What we do with your information:
  • To provide the service
  • To respond to your question
  • To identify you
  • To process your transaction
  • To ensure the security
  • To send periodic emails

3. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to, and we do not solicit any personal information from children under the age of 16. If you are under 16, please do not attempt to fill out our forms or send any personal information about yourself to us. If we become aware that a child under 16 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information from our files.

4. Security

We are committed to protecting the information received from you. We implement a variety of security measures to protect your personal information when you register an account, place an order or fill out the form. When you register on our website, we suggest that you do not save your password on your device or share it with any person. Besides, we will encrypt your personal information when you make your payment.

5. Contact Us

If you have question regarding any information in this Privacy Policy, please reach out Personal Data Protection Officer via Shome@gmail.com. You can also contact us via our customer service at our Site.
